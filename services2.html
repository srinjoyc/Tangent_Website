For Clients

Tell us about your project

Book a free initial meeting with us and we will answer your questions to uncover your project needs. If you know your tech talent needs, we will find a qualified developer for you!

Recieve an initial proposal

We will provide you with a proposal that outlines your project needs, how we can meet them, our commitment to you and a cost estimation for the services. A 24/7 project manager will be assigned to mediate between you and the developers, analysts, or designers.

UI/UX Design

To gage a specific understanding of your project needs, we will match you with a UI/UX designer. We will create a final proposal outlining your project team, exact cost and an overview.

Begin

You’re ready to work with your new project team, risk free! Receive a team of developers for the price of a freelancer.

To Join The Collective

Apply to be part of the team

Whether you’re a self-taught developer or an industry professional, you can be part of the collective. Send us your resume and personal information through our website or at relations@tangentcollective.com

Interview with us

We will shortly send you an email with a coding challenge and an interview date. Everyone gets an interview regardless of experience, and sometimes we provide learning opportunities for willing individuals. Depending on your skill level, you might be required to complete an internal examination.

Hiring package + NDA

Accepted candidates will receive a hiring package and NDA form which they are required to fill out prior to starting. Once we are in the final stages of acquiring a project, you will be contacted with a potential work agreement.
