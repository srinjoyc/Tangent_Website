About Us

We take the headaches out of development for both the client and the developer.

We manage, consult, and contract.

As a non-technical individual, the process of ideation to production can seem overwhelming. We take you through step-by-step of building an app and provide as much or as little support as you need.

We serve your you business needs.

As a developer, you enjoy coding and most likely don't enjoy dealing with all the other headaches than can come with freelancing or doing contract work. For a small fee we can take care of all that, so you can do focus on what really matters.

Our Services

Full Stack Web Develompent

Choose from either in-house, outsourced, or contracted developers. We put together a the team that makes sense for you project.

Mobile Development

Similar to our web development, we can do mobile development through technologies such as React or natively on iOS and Android.

Market Research

Does your tech stack, app idea, or feature set line up with market needs? We can do the research to find out. This includes both primary and secondary research as well as customized packages tailored to your needs.

Management

Past, current or future - we provide a technical product manager to keep up with your product and your team. The manager will perform set duties to ensure your time is spent on areas that need it the most without compromising any other part of the project.

Rapid Prototyping

Need to test a concept fast and cheap? We put together a plan to hash out an MVP at the lowest cost and a relatively quick timeline

Design

Logos, branding, UI/UX, and wireframing. We can do this independently or in coordination with a larger project, you choose the time and money you would like to spend, we find the best fit.

Looking to kickstart or upgrade your project?

We strongly believe in matching business objectives with the resources required to achieve it. Let us prepare a proposal for you to explore the possiblities. Within a week, you will have a roadmap to success.
